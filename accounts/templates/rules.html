RULES

      • Each team can consist of maximum of 3 members.
      • The quiz will contain 30 questions.
      • Each question will be of 1 mark. So, the team will be scored out of 30 marks.
      • The duration of the test will be of 30 minutes. It will get submitted automatically once the time is up.
      • All the questions can be attempted only once.
      • There is no negative marking in the quiz.
      • There is only one correct option.
      • Use of calculator or any electronic gadget is strictly prohibited.
      • Teams having score above threshold will be selected for Round 2.
      • Pressing the refresh or back button on the browser will result in automatic submission of the test.
