ABOUT US

  Cretronix is an electronics based competition which tests the participant's theoretical & practical knowledge. Whether you are a total non-techie, an electronics enthusiast, a software developer, or some other type of engineer, you will find this event very enthralling. Cretronix is a place where creativity meets electronics and thousands of doors open towards discoveries and innovation in the field of electronics and telecommunication.

   Participants have to go through two rounds, first round is online MCQ based round which consists of basic questions related to electronics and electrical. For the second round, participants will be given a problem statement and they are supposed to implement the solution with the help of hardware components(simulation softwares for senior round).

  So, let's get together and explore the fabrication of circuits.
